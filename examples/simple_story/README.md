## Story Summary

"Pepper and the Backward Dive" follows Pepper, a young penguin who is afraid to dive into the water while her friends practice for the annual jellyfish migration swim. With help from her grandmother (Gran-Gran), Pepper learns that trying scary things is possible - and worth it.
